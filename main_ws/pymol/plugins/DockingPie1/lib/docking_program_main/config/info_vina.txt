AutoDock Vina [1,2]

"AutoDock Vina is an open-source program for doing molecular docking.
It was originally designed and implemented by Dr. Oleg Trott
in the Molecular Graphics Lab (now CCSB) at The Scripps Research Institute [1]"

[1] https://vina.scripps.edu/
[2] O. Trott, A. J. Olson, AutoDock Vina: improving the speed and accuracy of docking with a new scoring function, efficient optimization and multithreading, Journal of Computational Chemistry 31 (2010) 455-461


AutoDock Vina in DockingPie

The binaries for macOS, Linux and Windows are directly provided in DockingPie source code.
By clicking the "Install" button, Vina will be made executable.
